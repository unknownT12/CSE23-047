ABOUT

"Inception" is a 2010 science fiction action film directed by Christopher Nolan and starring Leonardo DiCaprio. The movie takes place in a world where technology exists to enter and manipulate people's dreams. Dom Cobb (DiCaprio) is a thief with the rare ability to enter people's dreams and steal their secrets. However, his skills have left him a fugitive and cost him everything he has ever loved. Now, Cobb is offered a chance at redemption when he is given a seemingly impossible task: plant an idea is someone's mind, rather than steal one. With the help of a team of specialists, Cobb must navigate the dangerous and unpredictable world of dreams to complete the mission and regain his life.
